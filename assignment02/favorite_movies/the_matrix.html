The Matrix

The Matrix is a 1999 American science fiction action film that depicts a dystopian future in which humanity is unknowingly trapped inside a simulated reality, the Matrix, created by intelligent machines to distract humans while using their bodies as an energy source.

The main character is a computer programmer Thomas Anderson, under the hacker alias "Neo". He uncovers the truth, and "is drawn into a rebellion against the machines" along with other people who have been freed from the Matrix.

The movie has a 8.7 rating on IMDb.
